Void-scan:

Void-scan or vscan is my own network scanning tool written in Golang. It is still in it's initial stage, just stabilised it there is a lot of features to be added, it's still a good ports scanner for CTFs where speed is needed, it can complete a basic 65535 ports scan in around 1 to 2 seconds in berserk mode.
