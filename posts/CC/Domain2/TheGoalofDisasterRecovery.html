7. The Goal of Disaster Recovery

Disaster Recovery Planning (DRP)

DRP starts where business continuity ends.

Purpose of DRP

It guides actions after a disaster or major interruption.

Main Goal

Its goal is to restore the business to full reliable operation.

Focus of Disaster Recovery

It mainly restores IT systems and communication services.

Role During Disruption

It supports the organization during the disruption and recovery period.

Importance of IT Recovery

IT recovery is often essential for full business recovery.

Difference from Business Continuity

Business continuity keeps critical functions running, while disaster recovery restores IT and communications after disruption.

Simple Meaning

BC keeps the business running; DRP brings technology back to normal.
