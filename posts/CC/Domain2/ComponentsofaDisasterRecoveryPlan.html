8. Components of a Disaster Recovery Plan

Components of a Disaster Recovery Plan

A DRP includes different types of documents made for different people in the organization.

Executive Summary

Gives a high-level overview of the disaster recovery plan.

Department-Specific Plans

Each department may have its own recovery instructions based on its role.

Technical Guides

IT staff use technical guides to restore and maintain critical backup systems.

Full Copies of the Plan

Critical disaster recovery team members should have full copies of the complete plan.

Checklists for Disaster Recovery Team Members

Recovery team members use checklists to guide their actions during a disaster.

Technical Guides for IT Personnel

IT personnel use detailed guides to get alternate sites and systems running.

Manager and PR Documents

Managers and public relations staff need simple, high-level documents to communicate clearly during the disaster.

Simple Meaning

Different DRP documents are created for different roles so everyone knows what to do.
