7. Making Connections

Threat Category Example from Text CIA Element Impacted
Human / Social Password sharing between Joe and Joanne Confidentiality & Integrity
Physical / Remote Family members using an unlocked work laptop Integrity & Confidentiality
Environmental Power outages and mechanical generator failure Availability
Accidental Improper fire suppression methods Availability & Integrity

Explaination:

The table above tells about the connections between the type of threat and the CIA Triad.

  • First Human/Social: This affects Confidentiality and Integrity of the company, as it can be used by Red Teamers or Black hats to gain trust and get the Username and Password.
  • Second Physical/Remote: This is also similar to Human, but in this case, an attacker can install a malware or spyware and remotely access it, from afar.
  • Third Environmental: These cases like power outage and environmental failures.
  • Fourthly Accidental: These are caused by carelessness, by doing a bad job at not focusing on the quality.

These are the ones mentioned in CC official module, but there are more cases.
