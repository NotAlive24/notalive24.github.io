19. Non-repudiation

Non-repudiation is a legal term defined as the protection against an individual falsely denying having performed a particular action. It provides the capability to determine whether a given individual took a particular action, such as created information, approved information or sent or received a message. (This is mainly for E-commerce sites like Amazon).

Non-repudiation methodologies ensure that people are held responsible for transactions they conducted.
