9. Physical Security Controls

Physical Access Controls

Physical access controls are security measures you can physically touch. They are used to prevent, monitor, or detect direct access to systems, equipment, or areas in a facility.

Purpose

They prevent unauthorized people from entering physical locations such as workplaces or secure areas.

Protection of Assets and People

Physical access controls protect company assets, but their first priority is always the safety and security of people.

Main Idea

Physical security controls stop unauthorized physical access and help protect both personnel and physical assets.
