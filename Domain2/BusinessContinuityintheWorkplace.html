3. Business Continuity in the Workplace

While digital business continuity plans are convenient, they are useless if a disaster knocks out power or internet access. To stay safe, organizations keep a physical "Red Book", a hard-copy backup stored off-site by key staff members.

For this system to work, the paper copy must be updated every single time the digital version changes so that the instructions stay accurate during an emergency.
